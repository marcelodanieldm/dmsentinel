DM Sentinel Protocol

Technical guide on the audit process, mitigation, and risk scoring criteria.

Service Workflow

1

Global Payment Activation

The process is instantly triggered upon payment confirmation via Stripe, Mercado Pago, or Pix. Transaction metadata activates our engine through a Webhook.

2

Sentinel Engine: Deep Scan

Our Python engine performs non-invasive fingerprinting to identify CMS versions, active components, and exposed configuration files (.env, .git).

3

Intelligence Cross-Reference (CVE)

Detected versions are cross-referenced with our Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVE) database. A severity level is assigned to each finding.

4

Alerts and Reporting

If the Risk Score is below 70, a red alert is sent via Telegram. Data is consolidated into a JSON report for your Power BI dashboard.

5

Sentinel Academy Onboarding

Moodle access credentials are sent. The client's staff begins mandatory training to mitigate social engineering risks.

Risk Scoring Algorithm

The Sentinel Score starts at 100 points. Points are deducted based on the potential impact on data confidentiality and integrity.
